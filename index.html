About Me

Hello! I'm Jackie Yu, a self-taught front end developer based in Austin, TX. I am looking for an opportunity at an exciting company that will challenge and further my development as a junior developer.

After working in the architectural industry for the last few years, I wanted to apply my design skills and perspectives in a new direction. I was introduced to web development after working with an independent contractor to design, build and maintain Valcucine Chicago's website. Through this experience, it peaked my interest in web development and led to my decision in becoming a front end developer.

I hope to combine my technical knowledge with my keen eye for design to create engaging user experiences while writing clean and efficient code.

A few technologies I've been working in recently:
  • HTML
  • Javascript
  • CSS
  • Git
