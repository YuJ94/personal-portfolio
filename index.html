About Me

Hello! I'm Jackie Yu, a Full Stack Developer based in Austin, TX. I am currently attending Tech Elevator's National Live Remote cohort learning to develop dynamic web applications using the Java programming language and platform. Upon graduation, I hope to combine my technical knowledge with my keen eye for design to create engaging user experiences while writing clean, efficient, and accessible code. I am looking for an opportunity at an exciting company that will challenge and further my growth as I pursue this new career.

After working in the architectural industry for the last few years, I wanted to apply my design skills and perspectives in a new direction. I was introduced to web development after working with an independent developer to design, develop and maintain Valcucine Chicago's website for a previous employer. This experience piqued my interest in web development and led to my decision to explore the world of technology. Like Architecture, programming combines my creative thinking and problem-solving skills, turning my ideas into reality.
