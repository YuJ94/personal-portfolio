About Me

With 6+ years of professional experience, including 4+ years in architecture and 2+ years in software development, I bring a unique blend of skills to software development. My architectural background has sharpened my problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and project management capabilities, all of which are essential to be successful as a developer.

During my time in architecture, I collaborated on complex projects, consistently meeting deadlines, and exceeding client expectations. This experience proved invaluable as I moved into software development, where I have contributed to several innovative projects.
