About Me

Hello! I'm Jackie Yu, a Front-end Developer based in Austin, TX. I am looking for an opportunity at an exciting company that will challenge and further my growth as I pursue this new career.

After working in the architectural industry for the last few years, I wanted to apply my design skills and perspectives in a new direction. I was introduced to web development after working with an independent contractor to design, build and maintain Valcucine Chicago's website. Through this experience, it piqued my interest in web development and led to my decision in becoming a Front-end Developer.

I hope to combine my technical knowledge with my keen eye for design to create engaging user experiences while writing clean, efficient and accessible code.
